About Me

I was born and raised in the USA. When I was a teenager I studied in Barcelona and from that moment it was my goal to live and work in Europe.

For the past 20 years I have been working in the advertising industry. At first I worked at advertising agencies and then I began working at Facebook where I interacted with a variety of large advertisers. My experience at Facebook was especially enjoyable because I got to be part of the company as it grew from a startup to a large company. I was also able to transfer to the London office and fulfill my dream of getting back to Europe.

Currently I live in Sitges, Spain with my wife and 2 children. I am a happy person. I am taking a web development course because it is something I have always wanted to do and I am really enjoying it.
